Two philosophies of AI listing video

This isn't a features race — it's a genuine fork in what the video should be.

01 · The footage

Invented paths vs verified scenes

A continuous fly-through generates the camera's journey between your photos — doorways, hallways, and turns no camera captured. That's invented floor-plan footage by design. Vistalia animates each real photo as its own scene and never fabricates the space between them.

02 · The proof

"Looks right" vs receipts

Vistalia inspects every generated scene against its source photo, re-renders drift automatically, and publishes a per-video verification certificate — each scene beside the photo it came from. Show it to your broker, your seller, or your board.

03 · The voice

A visual vs a narrated tour

Soarly's product centers the fly-through visual. Vistalia's centers the story: a written narration voiced in your own cloned voice with word-synced captions — the video that sells you as the agent, not just the software.

Where Soarly genuinely makes sense

Fair is fair: Soarly is more than a video generator — it bundles listing prospecting. It scores video-less listings in a target area, drafts outreach, and attaches a demo video to help you win the client. If you're a videographer or a prospecting-heavy agent whose bottleneck is finding listings to pitch, that workflow has real value, and Vistalia doesn't do it.

Vistalia's whole bet is the opposite specialization: once you have the listing, the video itself should be the best and the most defensible on the block. Narrated in your voice. Every scene from a real photo, verified against it. Certificate included. One link of proof for anyone who asks.

Why the fly-through question matters more every month

As AI listing media gets scrutiny — from boards, brokers, and buyers who toured a home that didn't match the video — the question shifts from "does it look good?" to "can you show me it's accurate?" Continuous generation can't fully answer that question, because its most impressive frames are, structurally, inventions: the model's guess at your floor plan. Our hallucination field guide covers how to audit that footage if you use it. Vistalia's answer is architectural: we never generate the space between photos, so there's nothing to guess.

The easiest way to decide

Run the same listing through both. Vistalia's first video is free, no card — watch it come back narrated in your voice, then open the certificate and check every scene against your photos yourself. If a silent fly-through beats that for your business, you'll know in ten minutes.
