# Vistalia — Fastest Path to Profitability
*Written 2026-07-17 (day 5 post-launch). Built on verified numbers, not projections-from-hope.*

## The equation

Monthly costs at today's settings: **ads $4,500** ($150/day) + **fixed ~$300** (Render Pro Plus, Vercel, ElevenLabs, misc) + **trial COGS ~$400** (12% of 750 leads render a free 30s at ~$4.50). Total burn ≈ **$5,200/mo**.

Contribution margins (after COGS, fully-used worst case): **PAYG $39 → ~$35** · **Pro $69 → ~$47/mo** · **Studio $149 → ~$104/mo** · annuals = same margin, cash up front.

Break-even requires ~**104 Pro-equivalents at $150/day** ads — months away on cold paid alone. At **$50/day** it's **~24 Pro + ~21 PAYG**. Conclusion: profitability ASAP is not an ad-scaling problem. It is a **conversion-rate and cost-line problem**. Every point of trial→paid is worth more than any point of CPL.

## The four numbers that run the company

1. **CPL** (healthy band $5–8; investigate two days >8.5)
2. **Lead → first render** (the 6:40 nudge cohorts will reveal the true rate; day-4 evidence ~7–12%)
3. **Renderer → payer within 14 days** (target ≥15%; this is THE lever)
4. **Contribution margin vs. daily burn** (the profitability clock)

All four visible on /founder + morning readout. Ignore everything else, including follower counts.

## Phase 0 — Conversion machinery (this week, ~zero dollars)

The 750 leads/mo we already buy are worth 2–3× more with machinery that sells at the right moments. Renders end with a watermarked masterpiece and an agent at peak excitement — today we say nothing.

- **Post-render upsell email (T+20h after first render):** "Your video's ready for its listing — make one for your *next* listing in 10 minutes. $39, or 5/mo for $69. Watermark-free." One template + one nudge-style pass. *(Build: half day.)*
- **Credits-exhausted moment:** when the free/granted credits hit zero at Generate-click, the paywall should name the listing they were about to render. Losing a *specific* listing hurts more than losing a feature.
- **Tag flywheel:** render-complete email gains "Post it and tag @vistalia.ai — we feature our favorite listings." Every customer becomes distribution into their agent network. *(Build: 2 minutes.)*
- **Retargeting audiences now, spend later:** create Meta custom audiences (pixel visitors, lead-form openers, IG/FB video viewers) so they accumulate from day one. Warm audiences convert at a fraction of cold CPL.
- **Boost the live reel $15–20** at the Realtor job-title audience: seeds the account, social proof for every lead who checks whether we're real.

## Phase 1 — Right-size paid, add warm + wedge (weeks 2–4)

- **Cold leads: drop $150 → $75/day.** At $6 CPL that's still ~375 licensed agents/mo — more than the conversion machinery can chew — and it halves the burn clock. Scale back up only when renderer→payer ≥15% is *measured*.
- **Retargeting: $10–15/day** once audiences pass ~1,000. Creative: the verification angle ("checks every scene against your photos — MLS-safe") + PAYG $39 as the ask. Warm traffic is where the first cheap purchases come from.
- **Photographers ad set revival, $25/day test.** One photographer serves 10–50 agents (B2B2B multiplier); the dormant ad set + photog-hero creative already exist. Kill in 7 days if CPL >2× agent CPL.
- **Sell PAYG as the wedge, Pro as the graduation.** Agents think per-listing (commission-aligned). First dollar = $39 video for a live listing. After a 2nd–3rd PAYG purchase inside 60 days → personal-feeling email: "you've bought 3 — Pro would've saved you $48."
- **Annuals for cash:** Pro $490 up front covers ~3 days of ads each. Trial banner + paywall keep annual as default emphasis. Cash-flow positive can arrive before accounting-profitable.

## Phase 2 — Compounding channels (month 2+)

- **Customer-content engine:** repost tagged agent videos (permissioned) as reels; 1 reel/day cadence from showcase + customer content — the content factory costs pennies.
- **SEO shelf** (already live: compare/vistalia-vs-autoreel, roundup): add "AutoReel alternative," "Reel-E alternative," "AI listing video" intent pages. Reel-E built their whole company on this channel with $0 ads — it's slow, then it's free forever.
- **Facebook agent groups + broker partnerships:** value-first posts of finished tours; one brokerage pilot (Team/Brokerage tier exists in schema) = 10–30 seats in one sale.
- **Referral (build only after ≥10 payers):** give a free video, get a credit.

## Decision tripwires (pre-commitments, so 2 AM anxiety never decides)

- **CPL >$8.50 two consecutive days** → drop budget one notch, refresh creative.
- **Lead→render <8% after a full nudge cycle** → pause scaling; fix onboarding (the money is mid-funnel, not top).
- **Renderer→payer <10% by day 21** → the paywall moment is broken; run 3 price/copy tests before touching ad spend.
- **Renderer→payer ≥15% measured** → restore $150/day; consider $200.
- **Any week where contribution margin > (ads + fixed)** → profitable; scale spend 25%/wk while CPL holds.

## The honest timeline

Payers compound (subs stack month over month). At $75/day, realistic base case: **first paid dollars week 1–2** (Damon/Eileen-class users exhausting credits), **8–15 payers by day 30**, **contribution-positive around day 45–60**, cash-positive sooner if 2–3 annuals land. The variable that moves this most is renderer→payer — which is why Phase 0 ships before another dollar of spend scales.
