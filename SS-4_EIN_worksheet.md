# Form SS-4 — EIN Application Worksheet
### EstateMotion LLC (single-member LLC) — fax to the IRS

**How to use:** transcribe these onto the official Form SS-4
(irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fss4.pdf), then fax the completed page to
**855-641-6935** with YOUR fax number in the header so the IRS faxes your
EIN back (~4 business days). Fields marked **[YOU FILL]** need your info.

> Not tax/legal advice — this is the standard single-member-LLC filing.
> If you've elected S-corp/C-corp treatment (Form 2553/8832), Line 9a
> changes — you haven't mentioned that, so this assumes the default
> (disregarded entity). Confirm with your accountant if unsure.

## Fax checklist
1. Print/fill the SS-4 (one page).
2. Sign it.
3. Put your return fax number on the form (and in your fax cover, if used).
4. Fax to **855-641-6935** (domestic — US principal business).
5. EIN faxed back in ~4 business days. Save the confirmation — Stripe will
   want the EIN, and you'll want the CP-575 / fax confirmation on file.

## While the fax is in flight
Message Stripe and get them to state, in writing, the *exact* requirement
to lift the lock. If it's a risk-review hold rather than a missing EIN, the
new EIN alone won't release it — worth knowing before you wait 4 days.
