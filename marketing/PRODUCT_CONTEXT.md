## ONE-SENTENCE PITCH

EstateMotion turns a real-estate agent's listing photos into a polished cinematic listing video — the same caliber a $500 videographer would shoot — in under 5 minutes, with no editing skills required.

## ELEVATOR PITCH (90 words)

Every real estate agent needs listing videos. Hiring a videographer costs $300-$800 per listing and takes 3-5 days. Doing it yourself looks like a phone slideshow. EstateMotion sits between the two: upload your listing photos, pick a style (Luxury, Viral, MLS Clean, Investor), hit Generate. In under 5 minutes you get a 60-90 second cinematic video — vertical for Reels, square for Instagram feed, wide for YouTube — with AI-generated camera motion, animated address card opener, narrated voiceover, music, and your branded outro. Built for agents who post weekly listings, not annually.

## WHO IT'S FOR

**Primary persona:** Solo real-estate agent listing 2-15 properties per month. Sells homes in the $300K–$3M range. Posts to Instagram, Facebook, Zillow, and the local MLS. Has tried Canva and CapCut, gave up. Doesn't want to learn editing software. Will pay $79–$299/month for video that looks like the agent up the street paid $500 for it.

**Secondary persona:** Mid-sized brokerage (10-50 agents). Wants every listing to ship with a branded video without each agent having to produce one. Signs up the whole team on Cinematic AI 4K, controls the brand kit centrally.

**NOT for:** developers, large enterprise brokerages with in-house production teams, or hobbyists. Not for short-form video creators who already use Premiere or DaVinci.

## CORE DIFFERENTIATORS

1. **Speed:** under 5 minutes from photo upload to finished video. No competitor under $500/month is faster.
2. **One render → full bundle:** every render produces a vertical (Reels), square (feed), wide (YouTube/MLS), plus three pre-cut "social shorts" optimized for individual platform algorithms. Competitors typically charge per format.
3. **MLS-compliance mode:** the only AI video tool with a "compliance" mode that disables all generative motion on regulated room types (kitchens, bathrooms) where AI hallucinations would be a misrepresentation liability. Brokerage admins can enforce this.
4. **Real AI motion via Runway Gen-4 Turbo (or Gen-4.5 on premium):** actual image-to-video, not Ken Burns pan/zoom. Gives the "shot it on a steadicam" feel.
5. **Brand kit auto-applied to every render:** agent headshot in corner, name/brokerage outro card, contact line, license number. Once configured, every video ships on-brand without re-entering anything.

## VISUAL IDENTITY

**Brand color (primary):** `#0E0E10` (near-black, "ink")
**Accent color:** `#C7A76C` (warm gold, "Kodak film-print gold")
**Paper / cream:** `#F8F5EF` (warm off-white)
**Typography:**
- Headlines: serif (Domaine, Tiempos, Cormorant Garamond — luxury feel)
- Body: sans-serif (Inter)
- Small caps + extended tracking for labels (uppercase, letter-spaced 0.15em)

**Visual mood:** sophisticated, restrained, editorial. Think *Architectural Digest* video tour, not Zillow listing thumbnail. Dark backgrounds, gold accents, lots of negative space. Avoid: gradients, bright colors, exclamation marks, emoji.

**Logo mark:** italic capital "E" inside a square with a warm-gold-to-darker-gold gradient. The wordmark "EstateMotion" sits next to it in a serif weight 600.

**Imagery direction:** real-estate photography in twilight or golden hour. Architectural shots over interior shots. Aerial drone shots over street-level. People in shots are okay but should be back-turned or gestural, not posed.

## TONE OF VOICE

**Confident, not boastful.** "Built for agents who ship listings every week" not "The world's #1 listing video tool."

**Plain language.** "Upload photos, pick a style, hit Generate" not "Leverage cutting-edge generative AI to streamline your real-estate marketing workflow."

**Honest about tradeoffs.** "Renders take 3-5 minutes" not "instantly". "AI motion is occasionally imperfect on kitchen scenes — that's why we have a compliance mode" not "perfect every time."

**No hype words.** Banned: revolutionary, game-changer, magical, AI-powered (we are AI-powered, but we don't lead with it), 10x, ninja, unleash, supercharge, harness.

**Specific over general.** "10 finished videos per month" not "lots of videos." "Vertical 9:16 for Reels and TikTok, square for Instagram feed, wide for YouTube" not "all the formats you need."

## FOR HIGGSFIELD / IMAGE GENERATION SPECIFICALLY

When generating EstateMotion marketing imagery:

- **Aspect ratios:** vertical 9:16 (primary), square 1:1, wide 16:9
- **Lighting:** golden hour, twilight, dusk. Avoid harsh midday.
- **Subjects:** modern luxury homes (Spanish revival, contemporary, mid-century modern). Pool deck shots. Front exterior at dusk with lit interior windows. Kitchen island under pendant lighting. Open foyer.
- **NOT:** people in business suits, awkward staged shots, generic stock real-estate poses, "happy family" tropes, AI-generic faces, exterior shots in daylight with no shadow drama
- **Color treatment:** warm Kodak 2383-style print-film look. Gold push in highlights, slight teal in shadows. Think *Architectural Digest* color palette.
- **Composition:** clean lines, leading lines toward windows or vanishing points, layered depth (foreground / middle / background)
- **Text overlay style:** small caps, extended letter-spacing, gold accent color on dark background, serif headline + sans-serif sub

## FOR VIDEO GENERATION (Higgsfield, Runway, etc.)

- **Length:** 5-10 seconds per clip
- **Motion:** slow push-in, lateral pan, gentle parallax. NEVER whip pans, no rapid zooms, no shaky-cam.
- **Subject motion:** none preferred. Camera moves through still scenes.
- **Avoid:** people walking through frame, animated text, comic-book transitions, particle effects
