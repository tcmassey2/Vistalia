# EstateMotion — Higgsfield Ad Ideas v2

June 18, 2026. Built on what we learned this session: the **own-voice** feature is the
wedge Reel-E can't match, "first video free / pay-per-video" is the offer, and the
authentic-UGC style (no AI-slop) is what tested best. Format default: **9:16 vertical,
Meta/IG/TikTok, 15–25s, hook in the first 1.5s.**

Every concept follows your proven structure: **authentic phone-real hook → product reveal
(the cinematic mansion footage) at the flip/cut → offer card.** The hook should never look
AI-generated; the *reveal* is where Higgsfield's cinematic engine earns its keep.

## ANGLE A — Your own voice (the new wedge, lead with these)

### A1. "I haven't recorded a video in 6 months"
- **Hook (0–1.5s):** Agent talking to phone, casual, in car or kitchen: *"I haven't recorded a listing video in six months… but my sellers think I narrate every single one."*
- **Reveal:** Cut to the mansion walkthrough — and the narration playing over it is the agent's voice. *"Because I recorded my voice once. Now every listing sounds like me."*
- **On-screen text:** "Your voice. Every listing. Automatically."
- **CTA card:** "First video free → EstateMotion.ai"
- **Why it works:** curiosity gap + the exact benefit title companies are already DMing you about. Higgsfield job: only the mansion reveal clip.

### A2. The seller text
- **Hook:** Screen-record style — a text bubble lands: *"Just watched your video, your voice walking us through the kitchen made my wife cry 🥹."* Agent reaction, half-smile.
- **Reveal:** *"I recorded my voice one time."* → mansion clip in their voice.
- **On-screen text:** "They think you made it just for them. You kind of did."
- **CTA:** "Narrated in your voice. First one free."
- **Why:** social proof + emotional payoff. Cheap to produce (text overlay + one reveal clip).

### A3. "Pro voice or my voice?" A/B
- **Hook:** Same mansion clip plays twice. *"Most AI videos sound like this →"* [polished stock narrator]. *"Mine sound like this →"* [agent's own voice, warmer]. Agent: *"Guess which one wins the listing."*
- **On-screen text:** "Pro narrator OR your own voice. Your call."
- **CTA:** "First video free."
- **Why:** demonstrates the feature *and* the choice in one beat. Pure audio differentiation.

## ANGLE B — Silent reels vs. your voice (the Reel-E contrast, never name them)

### B1. "Stop posting silent reels"
- **Hook:** Blunt text-to-camera: *"If your listing videos are still just photos set to music… you're getting scrolled past."*
- **Reveal:** Mute icon over a generic moving-photo reel, then unmute → the mansion clip talking in the agent's voice.
- **On-screen text:** "🔇 Music ≠ a tour.  🔊 This is a tour."
- **CTA:** "Make one that talks. Free."
- **Why:** names the category weakness without naming the competitor. Pattern interrupt.

### B2. "The sound-on test"
- **Hook:** *"Turn your sound on. This is the difference between a $9 reel and a video that wins the listing."*
- **Reveal:** side-by-side — left: silent camera-pan-on-photo; right: EstateMotion mansion clip, narrated.
- **On-screen text:** "Sound on 🔊"
- **CTA:** "First video free → no subscription."
- **Why:** forces the unmute (engagement signal Meta rewards), and the contrast sells itself.

## FACELESS MODE — no founder face (use this)

Constraint: founder is 21, reads young, and doesn't want to be the face until there's
brand equity (established agents get skeptical/jealous of a young founder). Good news —
the strongest real-estate UGC is faceless anyway, and the own-voice product fits it
perfectly. Two routes:

### Route 1 — Zero-face, self-produced, $0 (do these first)
- **POV / over-the-shoulder phone.** Just hands + the phone screen. *"POV: you uploaded
  your listing photos and got this back in 3 minutes."* Re-casts C2 and any "agent" hook
  without a face. Most native-feeling format on IG/TikTok.
- **Screen recording demo.** Capture the actual flow — drop photos in → narrated mansion
  comes out. The product *is* the ad. Pairs with C1/C2/D1.
- **Text-thread / notification story.** A1 and A2 need no face at all — they're a text
  bubble from a "seller" + the reveal clip. Highest-trust, cheapest to make.
- **"Sound-on" contrast.** B1/B2 are pure footage (silent reel vs. narrated reel). No
  person needed; the unmute *is* the hook.
- **Text-on-screen + trending audio over property b-roll.** Bold hook as on-screen text,
  no voice, no face — let the mansion reveal and a trending sound carry it.

### Route 2 — Borrow a face that isn't yours (when you want a person)
- **Hire a UGC creator** who reads as an established 35–50yo agent — a relatable *peer* to
  your audience, which actually kills the jealousy problem better than being faceless. They
  shoot the car/desk/lender hooks on their own phone; you supply the script + the reveal
  clip. Sources: Billo, Backstage, Fiverr "UGC," or a local agent friend. ~$50–200/video.
- This is the move once a faceless ad proves an angle works and you want to scale it with a
  trusted-looking human reading it.

### On your voice
You can stay faceless *and* voiceless — text-driven ads need neither. If you want a voice,
don't use your own (it'll read young too); use a hired creator's, a neutral pro narrator,
or keep it text + music. Save "your voice" demos for showing the *product's* cloning
feature, where the voice is clearly a persona, not you.
