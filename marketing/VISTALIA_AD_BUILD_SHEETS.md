# Vistalia — Ad Build Sheets (CapCut-ready)

June 21, 2026. Second-by-second assembly for the first 4 launch ads. Each maps to a
rendered reveal in `marketing/higgsfield-ads/`. Build in CapCut/Premiere: drop the clip,
burn the on-screen text at the timecodes, lay the VO, add music, end with
`marketing/assembled/vistalia-endcard.mp4` (already branded with the V mark + offer).

**Global rules**
- 9:16, 1080×1920. Hook fully visible in first 1.5s. Total 15–22s.
- **One continuous music track across the whole ad** (don't restart at the flip) — duck it
  ~6 dB under the VO. Use a trending audio on TikTok, a licensed bed on Meta.
- **Voice:** do NOT use Troy's own voice (reads young — the lesson). Use a hired peer-agent
  creator's VO, a neutral pro narrator, or text-only. Save "your voice" for showing the
  product's clone feature, where the voice is clearly a persona.
- Captions: bold, high-contrast, lower-third, serif or clean sans. Keep 2–5 words per beat.
- Every ad ends on the branded end card (≈3s).
