Written July 6, 2026. Launch week is next week. 12 weeks on the clock.

## 0. The honest math first

500 subs at a ~$85 blended price (mix of $69 Pro / $149 Studio / annual-effective) ≈ **$42k MRR
from a cold start in 12 weeks**. $100/day of Meta ads alone gets you 100–150 subs by October,
not 500. The gap closes only if three engines run at once, every day, and one of them compounds:

| Engine | What it costs | Realistic contribution by Oct 1 |
|---|---|---|
| Paid ads (Meta, scaling to ~$300/day behind proof) | $12–18k total | 150–250 subs |
| Founder outreach: free render of THEIR listing (20/day) | ~$1/render + 2 hrs/day | 80–140 subs |
| Teams/brokerage deals (Studio, 5 pitches/wk) | time | 60–150 subs (lumpy — one 30-agent team is a step change) |
| Organic daily content + watermark loop + referrals | time | 40–80 subs |

**Base case if the plan is executed daily: 300–450. Hitting 500 requires the ads to scale clean
(CAC ≤ $60) AND at least two team deals landing.** The plan is built to make that possible and to
tell you by August 15 whether it's happening. Slipping to 500-by-November is a good outcome;
pretending the ladder is guaranteed is not.

**The one asymmetric weapon:** your product makes its own sales demo for ~$1 in 7 minutes.
Nobody else's outreach can include a finished cinematic video of the prospect's OWN active
listing. Engine 2 is the plan's soul — the ads make people aware; the personalized render closes.

### Unit economics guardrails (recheck weekly)
- COGS ≈ $6–8/video → Pro $69 gross margin ~50–60% at full usage.
- Allowable CAC early: ≤ $69 (one month). Scale ads only where CAC < $60.
- Free video COGS ≈ $6–8 → every 100 free signups costs ~$700. Activation (signup → first
  render) must stay > 55% or the free offer is leaking.

## 2. WEEKS 1–2 — Launch (day-by-day explicit)

**Every weekday, non-negotiable (the Daily Six — ~4 hrs):**
1. **Ads check (15 min, morning):** spend, CPC, CTR, cost/signup vs kill rule (CTR < 0.8% after
   $150 → kill ad; cost/signup > $12 after $200 → kill ad set element).
2. **Outreach (90 min):** 20 personalized contacts. 10 = full treatment (render their actual
   active listing, DM/email it). 10 = light touch (comment + DM on agents posting listings).
3. **Organic (45 min):** 1 post to IG Reels + TikTok + YT Shorts (same clip, native captions).
   Save every good render to the content bank.
4. **Support + activation (30 min):** reply to every user; personally message anyone who signed
   up but didn't render ("want me to run your first one for you?").
5. **Metrics (10 min, evening):** log signups / activations / subs / spend in the tracker row.
6. **Product (60 min cap):** ship the smallest thing users hit that day. Not features — friction.

## 3. WEEKS 3–12 — The operating system

Day-by-day stops being a list of dates and becomes a weekly template. Deviations happen in the
Friday review, nowhere else.

**Mon — Ads day.** Weekend data read; scale/kill per gates; 2 new creatives into rotation
weekly (best organic clip → ad). From Week 6: duplicate winners into a conversion-optimized
ad set (pixel has ~200 signups by then — enough signal).
**Tue — Teams day.** 5 new team/brokerage pitches + all follow-ups. Offer: Studio + white-glove
onboarding (you render their first 3 listings). One 20-agent team = a week of the ladder.
**Wed — Product day.** Biggest friction from the week's support threads. Ship it.
**Thu — Outreach surge.** Double outreach (40 contacts). Fall listing season (Sep) this becomes
the best-converting day of the week.
**Fri — Review vs ladder + content batch.** Metrics honesty hour: which engine added what, CAC
by channel, churn count and reasons (call every canceler personally through Week 8).
**Sat — 90 min:** schedule next week's posts. **Sun — off.**

**Scaling gates (mechanical, no vibes):**
- Ads: CAC < $60 for 7 consecutive days → +33% budget. CAC > $90 for 5 days → -50% and creative
  refresh. Never scale >33% at once (resets learning).
- Hiring the first VA (~Week 6, $800/mo): when outreach conversion is proven (>5% contact→sub),
  hand them the light-touch half. Your hours go to teams + product.
- TikTok ads (~Week 9): only after Meta CAC is stable; port the 2 winning creatives.
- Annual push (Week 11): Sep 21–28 promo — 2 months free on annual (already priced in at
  $490/$990). Converts fence-sitters before the ladder's final week.

**AUGUST 15 REFORECAST GATE (Week 5, hard checkpoint):**
- ≥ 120 subs: ladder holds. Scale ads aggressively, hire the VA, keep going.
- 70–119: 500 moves to Nov 1. Diagnose the weak engine, double the strongest one. No shame —
  this is still a real business growing fast.
- < 70: the funnel has a hole, not a volume problem. Freeze spend at $100/day, spend a week on
  activation + pricing interviews (call 20 users), then re-plan.
