# Higgsfield Prompt Pack — Problem-First Ads

*Jul 19, 2026. Rule of the pack: every ad sells the wound — the seller watching, the listing losing, the calendar that won't cooperate. The product appears only as the turn. Faceless throughout (hands, screens, houses, objects — never a presenter, never Marketing Studio). Generate clips in Kling via Higgsfield at 9:16; all text is OURS in post (never ask the model to render words — it garbles them). I assemble with the beat-cut recipe, brand chips, music, and end card once clips land in marketing/ads/raw/.*

**Prompt craft rules (apply to every prompt):** one action per clip, 5s, vertical 9:16, describe camera move explicitly, end every prompt with: `no text, no captions, no watermarks, no readable logos, no faces, photorealistic, natural motion`. Warm dusk-leaning palette to match the brand.
